Welcome Message From The Dean


The School of Computing and Information Technology has two departments i.e Department of Computer Science
and Department of Information Technology. Currently the School offers 2 PhD programmes, 2 Master
programmes, 5 undergraduate programmes, 3 Diplomas and 1 Certificate course. The School is a regional
leader in teaching and research for students in undergraduate and postgraduate levels.
